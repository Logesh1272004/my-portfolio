Comparative Performance Analysis of Neural Architectures for Poem Generation

This study delves into the impact of advanced neural architectures, including Bi-LSTM, Vicuna-1B, and GPT-2, on poetry generation. Using tailored prompts for genre and theme, the models' abilities to craft expressive and stylistically accurate poetry are evaluated. By comparing generated outputs against a curated dataset of poems using BLEU and ROUGE scores, the research offers valuable insights into computational creativity and the applicability of these architectures to poetry generation.

Comparative Study of Low-Light Image Enhancement Performance: DCE-Net vs. MirNetv2

This paper compares two state-of-the-art algorithms, DCE-Net and MirNetv2, for enhancing low-light image quality. DCE-Net leverages a cross-connection module for better layer-level feature complementation, while MirNetv2 applies a multi-scale feature extraction approach. Results on the LOL dataset highlight DCE-Net's superior performance in improving image contrast and brightness, making it a promising solution for applications in surveillance, night photography, and medical imaging.
